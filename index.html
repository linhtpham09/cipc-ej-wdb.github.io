California Workforce Development Boards
An Analysis by the California Immigrant Policy Center

What are Workforce Development Boards (WDBs)?

Workforce Development Boards (WDBs) are business led boards that serve an area. For this area, WDBs are required to

Identify job market needs and opportunities

Oversee one stop career centers

Provide job training programs

Promote and develop the local economy

Methodology

Found and read each board's policies on work authorization and language Access.

Contacted boards whose policies were not available on the website.

Identified struggles and obstacles in community reports and the 2019 summary reports.

Coagulated data into visualization and analyzed.
